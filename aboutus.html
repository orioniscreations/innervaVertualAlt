About the organisation

Innerva Virtual Inc is built off of years of research in long-term care settings, endeavoring to understand how to create 21st century solutions for the aging population as everyday care becomes more important while the population ages. Virtual reality offers many opportunities to improve health in terms of cognitive, emotional and even physical wellbeing and is a growing approach to research and study across the world. Innerva Virtual Inc's mission is to drive development in software so as to provide powerful new tools to seniors, caregivers, clinicians and researchers.

Problem we are solving

There is a dearth of resources and gaps in care in the aging care industry. Too many aging seniors living at home and in care centers feel isolated, alone, disengaged, and far from specialist resources (such as PT/OT) unless in an urgent care situation. They also happen to have been more or less left out of the third industrial revolution culturally. This needs to be addressed. We build VR with seniors in mind. Standardized assessment tests for gait/balance as well as cognitive tests that measure memory/attention. Rehabilitative exercises and routines are taught in VR space, engaging the user and teaching as they go along. While they do this the IVI VR platform also records Headset+body motion in 3d space, capturing a snapshot of the user during the activity that can be reviewed remotely, and at another time by a caregiver. The platform is meant to provide multiple vectors of health and wellness that the user may engage in, and capture longitudinal data that could lead to preventative measures and avoid falls/fractures or the development of other serious conditions.
