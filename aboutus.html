About the organisation

Innerva Virtual Inc is built off of years of research in long-term care settings, endeavoring to understand how to create 21st century solutions for the aging population as everyday care becomes more important while the population ages. Virtual reality offers many opportunities to improve health in terms of cognitive, emotional and even physical wellbeing and is a growing approach to research and study across the world. Innerva Virtual Inc's mission is to drive development in software so as to provide powerful new tools to seniors, caregivers, clinicians and researchers.
