Virtual Reality Software for Healthy Aging

Innerva Virtual Inc. started from research done on the effects of virtual reality on mood in long-term care adults. What began as a curiosity into an emergent technology, turned into development of innovative solutions in long-term care health services. Our current mission is to identify gaps in health services in long-term care, and address them using these technologies to provide quality health services for aging adults across the world, ultimately maintaining and improving their wellness. Our mandate is to provide accessible preventative health services for the aging population.
